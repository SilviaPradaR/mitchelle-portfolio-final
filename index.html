Why hire me?

My name is Mitchelle Mora, I am a professional in Social Communication - Journalism with more than 6 years of experience in the educational sector, developing my skills as a communicator in international events, combining communication, design and IT support. I am currently studying Interactive Media Design at Seneca College in Toronto, Canada. Thanks to this, I have been able to offer my clients not only a wide range of services, but also certified experience in which I have developed competencies in softwares such as Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, HTML, CSS and Javascript and After Effects. I am passionate about higher education and how, through technology, we can bring us closer to it and facilitate the learning process.
